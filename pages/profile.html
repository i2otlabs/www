iSquared OT Labs was founded as a technology startup in 2016 with a vision of providing quality IoT Solution to various business in manufacturing, health care, pharmaceuticals and automobile segments.

Internet of Thing IoT is one of the most promising concepts that is evolving today. It is not just a piece of software or hardware, it is a tailor made solution for achieving better automation, optimization and control.

As an IoT solution provider, we partner with various business establishments. To name a few, we have IBM, PTC and few others with whom we partner. We provide solutions on both cloud and on premise platforms.

The type of IoT solution we provide today, falls in the space of data acquisition and analysis or more specifically to do with understanding the data that is collected from machines.

In addition to this we provide training and consulting in the IoT space for our customers. This includes in-house training and online training for outstation participants.

We work with a variety to customers in providing automation solution based on IoT. Currently our customers are from the manfucaturing and consulting space.
